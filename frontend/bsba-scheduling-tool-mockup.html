Assumptions & methodology

Requirement type (GEM classification)
Course requirement labels (major / gen-ed / elective) pool all 10 BSBA concentrations together. A course required in one concentration but not another is still labeled by its most common role across the whole freshman cohort — this view does not distinguish between concentrations.
Meeting time patterns
Meeting times reflect the most common post-census (after 2025-10-20) section pattern from historical terms. They are informational only and have not been checked for time conflicts against each other.
